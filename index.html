About Us

At Global Essence Exports, we connect India’s rich heritage with the world. From premium spices to authentic handicrafts, our product range embodies the essence of Indian craftsmanship and industry. Our company deals with spices, furniture, and handicrafts.

We are certified by SPICES BOARD OF INDIA to export Indian spices to world-wide nations without compromising on the standards. Our management strives to set benchmark for efficiency in executing spice business. We sterilize spices to control micro contamination up-to the required levels. We are assured about the prompt delivery and quality of products.

Spices: Our spices are sourced directly from farmers who adhere to sustainable farming practices. From the bold aroma of cumin to the vibrant hue of turmeric, each spice is carefully selected to ensure the highest quality. We take pride in delivering spices that not only enhance the taste of your dishes but also contribute to a healthier lifestyle.

Handicrafts: Our collection of handicrafts showcases the intricate workmanship of skilled artisans. From detailed pottery to elegant textiles, every piece is a testament to India's rich cultural heritage. We aim to support local communities by promoting their crafts on a global platform, ensuring that these traditional skills are preserved for future generations.

Furniture: Our handcrafted furniture is designed to combine aesthetics with functionality. Each piece is crafted with precision and care, using high-quality materials that ensure durability and beauty. Whether you are looking for a statement piece for your living room or practical solutions for your office, our furniture adds a touch of elegance and sophistication to any space.
